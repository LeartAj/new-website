Layout & Structure

Full-width viewport layout with centered content
Navigation bar positioned at the top center of the page
Small dotted pattern background covering the entire viewport

Color Scheme (Light Mode)

Background: Off-white or light cream (#FAFAFA or similar)
Dots: Very light gray (#E0E0E0 or similar) for subtle texture
Navigation pills: White background (#FFFFFF)
Active/selected navigation item: Black background (#000000) with white text
Inactive navigation items: Black text (#000000) on white background
Logo: Black (#000000)

Color Scheme (Dark Mode)

Background: Near-black or dark gray (#1A1A1A or similar)
Dots: Dark gray (#404040 or similar)
Navigation pills: Dark gray background (#2A2A2A)
Active/selected navigation item: White background (#FFFFFF) with black text
Inactive navigation items: White text (#FFFFFF) on dark gray background
Logo: White (#FFFFFF)

Typography

Navigation text: Clean, modern sans-serif font (likely system font stack or similar)
Font weight: Medium (500) for navigation items
Text should be clearly legible with adequate contrast

Dotted Pattern

Small circular dots arranged in a regular grid pattern
Spacing: Approximately 30-40px between dot centers
Dot size: 1-2px diameter
Pattern covers entire background seamlessly
Pattern should be subtle and not interfere with readability

Navigation Component (PillNav)

Items: Home, Writings, Technical, Recommendations, About
Rounded pill-shaped buttons with consistent padding
Smooth hover transitions
Clear visual distinction between active and inactive states
Logo positioned to the left of navigation items
Logo should be circular (use something default)

Spacing & Alignment

Navigation bar: 20-30px padding from top of viewport
Horizontal centering of navigation group
Consistent internal padding within navigation pills (approximately 12-16px vertical, 20-24px horizontal)
